Macros-Nutrition Calculation

One of the keys to success in Fitness is nutrition. For a long time, the general beliefe was that having a calorie deficit was enought but that's not true. Whatever is you objective, if you want to lose weight, bulkp up, etc, you need to know the correct proportion of macro-nutrients you need to eat daily to reach your goals in the healthiest way and without compromising your health. As a Fitness professional, I will help you calculate your daily macros of: Protein, carbohidrates and fats.

It is important to have the proper diet adapted to you, your lifestyle and your exercise to help you achieve your goals easier and maintain the results in the long term in the healthiest way, also you can still enjoy delicious meals!. I will help you calculate the macro-nutrition you need with the following process:

  • Calculate your basal metabolic rate: Standardized equations are used to determine how much energy your body uses at rest, based on age, sex, height and weight.
  • Adjust for activity level: The basal metabolic rate is multiplied by an activity factor to increase calories based on your activity level.
  • Adjust based on fitness goals: If you want to lose weight, bulk up, etc..
  • Determine your macros: Calculate how much protein, carbohidrates and fats you need.
