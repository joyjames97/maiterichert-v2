About me

Combining my passion for exercise with education lead me to fall in love with the industry and wanting to help others understand and take control of their bodies and lives.

There is a lot of contradictory information, diets and "health hacks". I aim to help each and every one of my clients to find their own personal healthy balance and get them to reach their goals by educating them to better understand their bodies and how to reach their goals without unmaintainable diets and fake "health hacks".

I focus on balance, IIFYM, HIIT and strength training offering both flexible and customized nutritional coaching and tailored exercise programs, as well as a strong and efficient mental coaching to focus on the long term results.
