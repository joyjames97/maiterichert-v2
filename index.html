Frequently Asked Questions

  • Do you coach females only?

    Yes. My years of experience and acquired knowledge are based on female fitness and I come from a competitive background in the Bikini Fitness category. That is why I made the choice to coach only clients in a field I am the most comfortable and experienced in.

  • Why don't you have a generic 30-days or 12-weeks programs?

    Personally, I am not a fan of the 30-days or 12 week programs as they only help you lose weight for a short period of time but they are not permanent solutions that can be maintained in the long term. That's why my formula is individually tailored to you and your goals, and lasts exactly as long as you need it!

  • How quickly will I see the results of my training?

    Expect to feel the results of your training sooner than you see them. People who start a new exercise program and are consistent in getting their workouts done, typically can feel improvements in sleep, mood and energy levels within 2 to 3 weeks. Changes in body composition often take longer to notice; the more consistent you are with your workouts and the closer you adhere to your nutrition plan, the sooner the results will become noticeable (to you and to others too!). We'll try focusing on non-scale victories like how many more pushups you can now perform, how your favourite jeans fit, or how you look in the mirror.

  • Why don't you propose training-only formulas?

    Exercise is extremely important in achieving your fitness goals, however, the nutrition component can simply not be ignored. In fact, nutrition is key. I will help you understand macros, create a nutrition plan and will keep you accountable with your diet, which will help speed up your results and make you feel better overall. Whether we like to hear it or not, abs are made in the kitchen! I will also put emphasis on the mental factor to help you going through all these changes and challenging steps of your fitness journey. We are all capable of achieving so much things, all we need is the right mindset!

  • Can I quit anytime?

    My mission is to guide you through your fitness experience and help you achieve your goals. All of them. I look forward to teach you everything I know as fast and efficiently as possible, so that eventually, you can continue your fitness journey on your own. While I understand that some people may be more impatient than others, please understand that sometimes it is simply not about achieving your goals fast but about achieving them, period. Nonetheless, you are free to quit anytime you see fit or feel ready to do so. Simply send me an e-mail or app-message and we'll complete the ongoing month subscription.
