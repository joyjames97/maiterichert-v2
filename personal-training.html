Personal Training Package

Exercise and the proper nutrition can have a huge positive impact in our overall health and wellbeing and that's why is important to set healthy habits but I'm aware that it can be difficult. That's why I offer my experience and knowledge to help you set the right direction and the proper training adapted to your own body and lifestyle. Together we will be able to achieve great results and 100% of the merit will be yours!

I will help you set the training and help you to stay motivated and focused. Also, I will provide you with the proper diet depending on your Fitness goals and lifestyle to improve your training performance and see results sooner. The personal training package includes the following features:

  • Training: Individual workout plan, at home or at the gym, adapted to your goals and changing every 8 weeks to set variety and adapt to your body performance.
  • Mental: The most important factor to keep your results in the long term and to develop a healthy attitude towards this lifestyle, I will help to stay motivated and focused to help you set healthy habits.
  • Nutrition: Individual diet plan adapted to your fitness goals, food preferences (vegan, vegetarian, keto, etc), intolerances (allergies), and/or macros information to stay flexible.
  • Progression Tracking: I will track your progress through my fitness app to stay up to date with your progression and evolution to keep adapting your training to your new neccesities.
