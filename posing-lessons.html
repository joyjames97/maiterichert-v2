Posing Lessons

I'm a personal trainer and professional athlete specialising in online coaching, mentoring and lecturing. In 2018 I was the winner of a WBFF Bikini Pro International competition and this achievement encouraged my passion for posing and to perfect the art of stage presentation.

With my knowledge and experience, receive a personalised one to one posing evaluation during each session allowing you to develop into a more complete and competitive athlete. The posing lesson would cover:

  • Identifying areas in need of improvement.
  • Understand drills, skills and warm ups.
  • Exercises to enhance poses.
  • Federation specific poses and stage layout.
  • Learn transitions, choreography, stage walks and presentation requirements.

If you are ineterested or if you want to know more, don't hesitate to get in touch!
